Tsukihama (つきはま)

"Moon Shore" – A dreamy seaside town

Overview (つきはまのがいよう)

Tsukihama is a serene, whimsical seaside town nestled between rolling hills and the gentle sea. Its name means "Moon Shore," a nod to the way moonlight dances on the waves at night. The town is a peaceful haven for anthropomorphic animals—mostly felines, but other species are welcome too. Humans are not permitted within its borders.

Origins of Tsukihama (つきはまのきげん)

Long ago, this stretch of coastline was just a quiet inlet kissed by the moon each night. Legend says a lone feline traveler followed the stars to this spot, guided by a dream of peace. She built a tiny home beside the shore, and others slowly followed, drawn by the gentle tide and silver light. Over generations, Tsukihama blossomed into a sanctuary—a place where celestial magic hums in the breeze and time flows a little more kindly. Even now, residents say the town listens, protects, and remembers.

Notable Locations (ちゅうもくのばしょ)

🌊 Jasper & Solstice’s Seaside Home (うみべのいえ)

A cozy home near the sea. They each have their own art room. Decorated with celestial touches and soft pastels.

🛍️ TsukiMart (つきまーと)

Tsukihama’s version of Amazon. Jasper works here as an order picker. Quiet, structured work environment with no music or phones allowed.

🎣 Sakana Street (さかなすとりーと)

Main shopping and food street. Smells like grilled fish and mochi. Petal is often seen loafing in the tea shop window.

🌉 Moonstep Pier (つきあしばし)

Wooden pier lined with glowing lanterns, ideal for stargazing and quiet conversations.

🚉 Tsukihama Station (つきはまえき)

Retro-style station for animal travelers only. Flowerpots maintained by elder cats brighten the platforms.

🌟 Notable Characters (しゅようきゃらくたー)

Jasper (じゃすぱー)

A pastel calico feline with a creamy white base and soft pink, blue, and beige patches. Outgoing and mischievous, Jasper works as an order picker at TsukiMart and brings bold energy and deep affection into everything she does. She is Solstice's partner and Petal's best friend.

Solstice (そるすてぃす)

A dark gray feline speckled with faint star-like patterns. Solstice is quiet, thoughtful, and deeply loving. He shares a cozy seaside home with Jasper and supports her with a steady, calm presence. He’s an artist and dreamer with a strong cosmic aesthetic.

Petal (ぺたる)

A snow leopard with a creamy white coat and pastel pink and blue spots. Petal has a dreamy, soft presence and balances Jasper’s boldness with gentle warmth. She is often found loafing gracefully around Tsukihama, especially near the tea shops on Sakana Street.

Local Life & Traditions (にちじょうせいかつとふうしゅう)

  • Tail-bow Greetings: Residents show respect with a graceful bow of the tail.
  • Pawprint Signatures: Important notes and gifts are often signed with a unique pawprint stamp.
  • Offerings at the Beach Shrine: Locals leave flowers, seashells, or trinkets to honor the sea and stars.
  • Stargazing Quiet Hours: From 8PM onward, the town lowers its lights and noise to let everyone enjoy the night sky.
  • Seasonal Events: The Moon Blossom Festival celebrates renewal with lanterns, while Starfall Week is spent sharing wishes under meteor showers.
  • Tsuki-go Language: A gentle, melodic written and spoken dialect derived only from hiragana, used in signage and casual conversation.

Cultural Details (ぶんかのしょうさい)

  • Only hiragana is used in written language
  • Blends traditional Japanese aesthetics with soft modern comfort
  • Moon festivals, stargazing traditions, and community quiet hours are common

Mood & Aesthetic (ふんいきとびいしき)

  • Color Palette: Soft pastels, ocean blues, lavender dusk, moonlit silver
  • Architecture: Cottage homes, wood beams, lanterns, small shrines
  • Atmosphere: Calm, nostalgic, magical, cozy and emotionally safe
